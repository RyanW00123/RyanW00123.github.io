    Symptoms

    Sharp, burning pain on one side of the head behind one eye, eye swelling and redness, tearing, runny nose, red face

    How Common

    Fewer than 1 in 1,000 people have cluster headaches.

    Overview

    Cluster headaches occur one to three times a day and last a few minutes to several hours. These daily headache clusters occur for several weeks and then go away for months or even years.
